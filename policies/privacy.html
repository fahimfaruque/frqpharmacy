FRQ Technologies Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 1, 2023

Introduction

FRQ Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our patients ("you") who utilize our services after visiting their doctor. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, storage, and sharing of personal information provided to us through SMS interactions as part of our pharmaceutical care services.

Consent for Contact

By giving consent to your doctor, you agree to receive SMS communications from us regarding pharmaceutical care. We will only contact you for this purpose and within the scope of your consent.

Collection of Personal Information

Personal Information Provided by You:

  • Contact Information: We collect your mobile phone number from your doctor with your verbal consent to engage with you over SMS about your health.
  • Health Information: Conversations over SMS may include discussions about your health, prescribed medications, and other health-related information.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • SMS Records: We automatically collect records of our SMS conversations, including date, time, and content of the messages.

Use of Personal Information

Your personal information is used to:

  • Provide personalized pharmaceutical care and advice via SMS.
  • Address your questions and concerns regarding your health and medications.
  • Improve the quality of our services.

Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Personal information may be shared with:

  • Healthcare Providers: With your consent, we may share relevant information with other healthcare providers involved in your care.
  • Service Providers: We may engage service providers to support the delivery of our services, such as SMS platform providers. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality and data protection obligations.

Security of Personal Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of inaccurate information.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time. Upon withdrawal, we will cease to use and delete your personal information, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes and obtain your consent where required.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us.
